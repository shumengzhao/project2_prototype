WELCOME TO OUR EXPLORATION

(logo concept) The inspiration of Gotogether logo is from jigsaw and plane. The jigsaw strongly represents the close connection. And plane is the popular transportation for traveling. For the final logo, the simplified plane makes by the jigsaw, which means getting together to travel.
